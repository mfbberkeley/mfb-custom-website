About Us

Our mission

Consulting and capital for local growth.

We deliver consulting and microloan support focused on long-term, sustainable growth. Each semester, we take on a select number of consulting projects while working to expand financial access for local businesses through Kiva-backed lending. By pairing strategy with capital, we aim to create meaningful and lasting economic impact in our community.

Our history

Built at Berkeley since 2012.

MFB was founded in 2012 to support organizations and entrepreneurship in the local Bay Area.

Microfinance at Berkeley provides financial and strategic consulting to organizations across industries. Our members apply varied academic perspectives and analytical skill sets to deliver thoughtful, data-driven solutions.

What we stand for

Core values

Empathy

We prioritize listening to and learning from the stories of local business owners, aligning our teams closely with our clients' goals and long-term success.

Community

We are passionate about contributing to the local business community by sharing knowledge drawn from our collective personal and professional experiences.

Integrity

We act with integrity by choosing what is right over what is easy, holding ourselves to high standards of honesty, accountability, and transparent communication.
