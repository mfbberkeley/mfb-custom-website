ABOUT

We deliver consulting and microloan support focused on long-term, sustainable growth. Each semester, we take on a select number of consulting projects while working to expand financial access for local businesses through Kiva-backed lending. By pairing strategy with capital, we aim to create meaningful and lasting economic impact in our community.

HISTORY

MFB was founded in 2012 to support organizations and entrepreneurship in the local Bay Area.

Founded in 2012, Microfinance at Berkeley (MFB) provides financial and strategic consulting to organizations across industries. Our members apply varied academic perspectives and analytical skill sets to deliver thoughtful, data-driven solutions.

CORE VALUES

Empathy

We prioritize listening to and learning from the stories of local business owners, which closely aligns our teams to our clients' success.

Community

We share a passion for contributing to the local business community and sharing knowledge from our vast personal and professional experiences.

Integrity

We act with integrity by choosing what is right over what is easy and never compromising our values. We hold ourselves to a high standard of honesty and accountability, grounding our work in evidence and transparent communication with our clients.
