Our impact

Who we are

Student-led consulting since 2012.

Founded in 2012, Microfinance at Berkeley is a student-run, nonprofit business organization that delivers finance and consulting services to organizations across the private sector. Our team of UC Berkeley undergraduates is passionate about fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and long-term impact.

Our story

What we do

Consulting, professional growth, and a community that lasts.

Our work

MFB provides consulting services to organizations at different stages of growth, helping small businesses access loans while supporting innovation and long-term success.

Develop professionally

Members gain hands-on experience through semester-long consulting projects, structured new-member training, alumni-led workshops, and technical skill development.

Create friendships

MFB fosters a close-knit community where members build meaningful connections through shared experiences, collaboration, and friendships that last beyond college.

Where we work

Internships and full-time placement.

A decade of alumni building careers in consulting, finance, and tech, and giving back to the next class along the way.
